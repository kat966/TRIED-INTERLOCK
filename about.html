Welcome to TRIED INTERLOCK®, where anime meets fashion in the most unique and stylish way! I'm Katleho Jankie, the creator and designer behind every piece of clothing you see here. As a lifelong anime enthusiast, I’ve always been inspired by the vibrant colors, bold designs, and iconic characters that bring anime to life. My passion for blending these influences into wearable art has led me to create a collection that speaks to those who want to express their love for anime in their everyday wardrobe.

Each piece is carefully designed and handmade, combining high-quality fabrics with attention to detail to ensure that you not only look good but feel amazing too. Whether you're channeling your favorite anime hero or adding a touch of kawaii to your outfit, my designs are meant to bring a little bit of that anime magic into the real world.

At TRIED INTERLOCK®, I believe fashion is about expressing yourself, and anime is the perfect inspiration for that. Thank you for supporting a small, passionate business and for embracing the world of anime-inspired fashion!
